Multigenerational Explorations Service: Finding & Creating Moments of Beauty In the midst of the trauma, grief and stress of the COVID 19 pandemic, people around the world are finding ways to create and acknowledge small moments of beauty. This Sunday for our monthly multigenerational worship service we will lift up & celebrate the beauty that exists even in times of crisis. We are adapting our usual Explorations format to move it online with Zoom. We will begin and end worship together, with an opportunity in the middle for participants to choose an Exploration activity that best matches their learning style.

Exploration in Movement: Dance Party! Join Rev. Alice in celebrating the beauty of dance and music as creative expressions that bring us joy and reduce stress. No experience or ability necessary!

Exploration in Conversation: Sharing Circle Join Cynthia Menard in a facilitated conversation focused on the question: Where are you finding beauty in your life right now?

Exploration in Spiritual Practice: Journaling Through a brief meditation and some journaling, we will be exploring our struggles and our moments of gratitude during this time of isolation and uncertainty. Led by Elisa Abatsis.

UNISON AFFIRMATION

Love is the doctrine of this church. The quest for truth is its sacrament and service is its prayer. This is our great covenant: To dwell together in peace, to seek the truth in love, and to help one another—to the end that all souls shall grow in harmony with the divine.

EXTINGUISHING THE CHALICE

We have gathered in love, worshiped in hope and lifted our hearts in peace. We go now to share these blessings with the world.
